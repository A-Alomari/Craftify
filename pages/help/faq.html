Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? add remove
To create an account, click the Sign Up button in the top right corner. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email. Once verified, you can complete your artisan or shopper profile.
How do I track my order? add remove
Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your status directly from your Dashboard under the "My Purchases" section.
What payment methods are accepted? add remove
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For high-value auction items, we also support bank wire transfers. All transactions are encrypted and secured.
How do I return or exchange an item? add remove
Returns are managed on a per-artisan basis as many items are custom-made. Please check the "Shipping & Returns" tab on the product page. To start a request, go to your order details and click "Request Return".
How does the auction bidding system work? add remove
Our auctions use a proxy bidding system. You enter the maximum amount you're willing to pay, and we'll automatically bid on your behalf up to that amount to keep you in the lead. If you're outbid, you'll receive an instant notification.
How do I become an artisan seller? add remove
We love new talent! Apply through our Maker Application. You'll need to submit a portfolio or photos of your work. Our curation team reviews applications within 5 business days to ensure quality standards.
